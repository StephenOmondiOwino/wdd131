Site Plan for StudySync

A collaborative study hub for university students.

Site Name

StudySync - This name represents a platform where students can sync their study goals, find resources, and collaborate with peers. The name is catchy, modern, and directly reflects the site's purpose.

Optional domain availability: studysynchub.org

Site Purpose

The website is designed to provide a collaborative platform for university and college students. Users can access shared study materials, join discussion boards, and track study goals. A registration form will be available for users to create accounts and join study groups.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find past exams and study guides for my Computer Science course?
  • How do I join or create a study group for Data Structures and Algorithms?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color (Navy Blue #1E3A8A): Used for headers, navigation bar, and buttons.
  • Accent Color (Light Cyan #E0F7FA): Used for background highlights, cards, and hover effects.
